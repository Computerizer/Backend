Mission:

Computerizer aims to be your companion in your computer building journey, helping you get started by providing tutorials and articles that solve both common and part specific problems. The elephant in the room is the pc building tool, that walks you through an easy to follow step by step process to buy your dream computer. At Computerizer we put our users and clients first, so instead of only suggesting you parts and products that fit your need, we do so while bringing you the best prices and highest discounts across the internet from established platforms.
